Privacy Policy

App: Battery Remote  |  Developer: MISMOtech Minos Eigenheer  |  Last updated: May 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MISMOtech - Minos Eigenheer ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Battery Remote application.

1. Information We Collect

Account & Authentication Data

  • Email address, display name, and profile photo (when signing in with Email, Google, or Apple).
  • Apple Sign In may share a relay email address instead of your real email at your discretion.

Location Data

  • The app retrieves the location of your tracker device via a Particle Cloud publish event.
  • Particle Cloud publish events are transient (TTL ~60 seconds in the cloud event stream)
  • Location information and device vitals are retained by Particle for up to 1 month.

Device & Usage Data

  • Battery level, charging state, warnings, and other telemetry data transmitted from your device.
  • Logs and events you enable in the app (e.g. charge sessions, warnings, motion events...) get published to a Firestore database.
  • Logs are stored for 90 days and can be accessed by the owner of the device and all users you grant access to.
  • We do not collect any personally identifiable information from these logs.

Push Notifications

  • A Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token is stored to deliver push notifications (e.g. charge complete, warnings).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and secure access to your account.
  • To display real-time and historical battery data on your dashboard.
  • To send push notifications about your device's status.
  • To improve app reliability and diagnose technical issues.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), protected by Firebase security rules that restrict access to your account only. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

4. Third-Party Services

Battery Remote uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database, cloud functions, push notifications)
  • Google Sign-In
  • Apple Sign In
  • Google Maps (for displaying device location)
  • Particle Industries (IoT device cloud — event transport and device vitals, retained up to 1 month)

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.

6. Children's Privacy

Battery Remote is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above.
